FAQ
  • What is Money Magnet?

    Money Magnet is a tool for budgeting and expense tracking to help you manage your finances and achieve financial goals.

    Sign up, link your bank accounts, and set up your budget categories.

  • Is my data secure with Money Magnet?

    Yes, we use bank-level encryption to ensure your data is safe.

  • How do I change my password?

    Go to account settings in your profile and select "Change Password."

  • How do I create a budget?

    Navigate to the "Budget" section, create a new budget, and set your income and spending limits.
